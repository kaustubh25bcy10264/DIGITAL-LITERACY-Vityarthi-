# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students:
-------------------------------------

## ✅ Do’s-
### 1. Think before posting: 
Share content that reflects positively on your character and professionalism.

### 2. Respect privacy: 
Adjust privacy settings and avoid oversharing personal information.

### 3. Credit sources: 
Give proper attribution when sharing academic or creative work.

### 4. Maintain professionalism: 
Use appropriate language and tone, especially on platforms visible to faculty or recruiters.

### 5. Engage responsibly: 
Participate in discussions constructively and avoid spreading misinformation.

-------------------------------------

## ❌ Don’ts-
### 1. Don’t post impulsively: 
Avoid sharing content when angry, upset, or without reflection.

### 2. Don’t plagiarize: 
Copying others’ work without credit is unethical and can harm your reputation.

### 3. Don’t overshare: 
Avoid posting sensitive details like addresses, phone numbers, or academic records.

### 4. Don’t blur boundaries: 
Keep personal jokes or memes separate from professional/academic platforms.

### 5. Don’t engage in cyberbullying: 
Never use social media to insult, harass, or demean others.

-------------------------------------
